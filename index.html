About company

The Mumbai Aircon Engineers,

referred to as MAE, was established in April 2021 in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra. With a team of experienced and certified technicians who specialize in design, installation, maintenance, and repair of HVAC systems, MAE has grown to become a leading provider of HVAC solutions in the region.

We pride ourselves on being able to provide the best service at while maintaining high standards of customer satisfaction. MAE services are designed to meet the needs of residential, commercial, and institutional customers. We have exclusive dealerships for Toshiba, Carrier, Midea, DRI, Mitsubishi and General.

Why choose us

Quality

We believe that the best way to provide our customers with quality service and products is by providing them with the best people who will do their job right every time.

Integrity

We believe that honesty and integrity are key values that define who we are as a business, both internally and externally. We will always act in accordance with these values, even when it may not be convenient or popular for us to do so.

Service

We believe in providing superior service to all customers, whether they are large or small, new or old; when they need something done right, we do it right first time!

Our Services

MAE is committed to providing excellent customer service at all times through effective communication, coordination and followup throughout the project lifecycle from planning through completion.

MAE specializes in providing a wide range of HVAC services, ranging from HVAC design to installation, maintenance, and repair. The company's services include heating systems, air conditioning systems, ventilation systems, and refrigeration systems.
